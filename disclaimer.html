DermaMind AI: A Supporting Tool, Not a Diagnostic One

DermaMind AI is designed to be an **assistive tool** for preliminary skin analysis and educational purposes only. Our advanced algorithms provide insights and identify potential patterns, but they are **not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.**

The information and analysis provided by this platform should not be used to self-diagnose or self-treat any health condition. **Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare professional, such as a dermatologist,** for any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or seen on this platform.

Accuracy and Limitations

While DermaMind AI strives for high accuracy (e.g., "99.2% accuracy" mentioned on the homepage refers to our internal model validation), the results are based on visual input and algorithmic interpretation. Factors such as image quality, lighting, and rare conditions can affect the analysis. The platform cannot account for all clinical factors a doctor would consider.
