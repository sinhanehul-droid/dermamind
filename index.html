About DermaMind AI

DermaMind AI is your personal assistant for preliminary skin health analysis. Our platform uses advanced AI models to help you understand your skin concerns by analyzing images and patient information. It is designed to be a first step, not a definitive diagnosis, empowering you to have more informed conversations with your dermatologist.

How It Works

Getting your analysis is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to get started.

1. Capture & Upload

Securely upload a clear photo of the skin area of concern.

2. Provide Context

Answer a few questions about your symptoms and history to give the AI more data.

3. Get Your Report

Receive an instant AI-powered analysis with findings and a risk assessment.
